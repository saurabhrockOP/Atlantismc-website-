Voting FAQ

help

I voted but didn't receive my rewards?

Make sure you entered your Minecraft username EXACTLY as it appears, including capitalization. Also ensure you are typing /reward in-game. If issues persist, the voting site API might be delayed by a few minutes.

help

How often can I vote?

You can vote on all 4 sites once every 24 hours. The timer resets based on the time you cast your vote on that specific website.

help

Do offline votes count?

Yes! You can vote while at school or work. Your rewards will be saved in our database safely until you log in and claim them.
