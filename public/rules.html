Official Server Laws

Ignorance of the rules is never an excuse.

1. Client Modifications & Hacking

The use of hacked clients (Meteor, Wurst, etc.), x-ray texture packs, auto-clickers, or any external macros that provide an unfair combat advantage will result in an immediate, unappealable IP and Hardware ban. Approved mods: Optifine, Sodium, and minimaps (without player radar).

2. Griefing, Raiding, and Lifesteal PVP

Raiding and stealing are strictly ALLOWED. This is a Lifesteal SMP; protect your base and your hearts. However, using illegal game exploits (like Bedrock breaking, TNT duping machines that crash TPS, or lag machines) to access bases is strictly forbidden.

3. Chat Conduct & Community Respect

Keep trash-talk related to the game. Extreme harassment, doxxing, releasing real-life information, DDoS threats, or using racial/homophobic slurs in the chat or Discord will result in permanent mutes or network bans. Respect the staff.
