Official Server Laws

1. Zero Tolerance on Hacked Clients

Use of hacked clients (Meteor, Wurst), x-ray texture packs, or auto-clickers results in an immediate, unappealable IP/Hardware ban. Approved mods: Optifine, Sodium, minimaps (no player radar).

2. Lifesteal Combat Mechanics

Raiding and stealing are strictly ALLOWED. However, using illegal game exploits (like Bedrock breaking, TNT duping that crashes the 20 TPS server) to access bases is strictly forbidden.

3. Chat Conduct

Keep trash-talk game-related. Doxxing, DDoS threats, or extreme harassment in the server chat or Discord will result in permanent mutes or network bans. Respect the staff.
