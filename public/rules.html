Official Server Laws

Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. Read them carefully.

1. Zero Tolerance on Hacked Clients

The use of unauthorized client modifications including, but not limited to, hacked clients (Meteor, Wurst, Aristois), x-ray texture packs, auto-clickers, kill-aura, or reach macros is strictly prohibited. Detection will result in an immediate, unappealable permanent hardware/IP ban.

Approved mods: Optifine, Sodium, Lithium, and minimaps (must not feature player/entity radar).

2. Lifesteal Combat & Griefing Mechanics

AtlantisMC is a competitive environment. Raiding, stealing, and killing players for their hearts are completely ALLOWED.

However, causing intentional damage to server performance is illegal. Using TNT duping machines to crash the server TPS, creating massive lag machines, or utilizing Bedrock-breaking exploits to access unraidable bases will result in an instant faction wipe and ban.

3. Chat Conduct & Respect

Trash-talking during PvP is allowed, but it must stay within the game. Doxxing, revealing real-life information, making DDoS or death threats, or using extreme racial/homophobic slurs in the Minecraft chat or Discord will result in immediate removal from the network. Respect the staff team; they are here to keep the server running smoothly.

4. Real Money Trading (RMT) & Scamming

Scamming other players using in-game currency or items is allowed (it's part of the survival experience). However, trading in-game items, hearts, or bases for Real Life Money (PayPal, FamPay, UPI) is strictly prohibited. Only official purchases via this webstore are permitted.

5. Alternate Accounts & Ban Evasion

You are allowed a maximum of two accounts per household. Using alternate accounts to bypass an active mute, temporarily ban, or permanently ban is a severe offense. If caught ban-evading, all associated accounts and IP addresses will be permanently blacklisted.

6. Store Policy & Chargebacks

By purchasing a rank or crate key from the AtlantisMC Webstore, you agree to our strict 1-hour refund window. Because digital items are delivered and used instantly, we do not tolerate fraudulent chargebacks. Attempting to refund a payment through your bank after using the items will result in an automatic, unappealable ban from our network and affiliated partner networks.
